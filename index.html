How to Play

Overview

Match cards by color, number, or symbol. Be the first to empty your hand!

Setup

Each player starts with 7 cards. A card is flipped from the draw pile to start the discard pile.

On Your Turn

  • Play a card that matches the top discard by color, number, or symbol
  • If you can't play, tap DRAW to take a card (you can play it if it matches)
  • Wild cards can be played anytime — choose the next color

Action Cards

  • Skip — Next player loses their turn
  • Reverse — Reverses play direction
  • Draw Two — Next player draws 2 and loses their turn
  • Wild — Play anytime, choose the color
  • Wild Draw Four — Choose color, next player draws 4 and loses turn

UNO!

When you have 1 card left, tap UNO! If you forget before the next player goes, you draw 2 penalty cards.

Scoring

  • Number cards = face value (0-9)
  • Skip / Reverse / Draw Two = 20 points each
  • Wild / Wild Draw Four = 50 points each

The round winner gets points from all other players' remaining cards. First to the target score wins!
